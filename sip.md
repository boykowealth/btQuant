# SIP Global  
## Systematic Index Partners

### Overview

**SIP Global (Systematic Index Partners)** is a quantitative proprietary trading firm focused on fragmented physical commodity markets.

The firm applies systematic trading discipline, quantitative pricing models, and structured market intelligence to physical assets where formal financial infrastructure is limited or nonexistent.

SIP operates primarily as a **trading shop**, while building internal market infrastructure to create persistent informational and execution advantages.

---

## Core Thesis

Many small and regional physical commodity markets lack:

- Transparent price discovery  
- Standardized contracts  
- Centralized liquidity  
- Reliable market data  
- Institutional participation  

These inefficiencies create large bid/ask spreads and structural arbitrage opportunities.

SIP exploits these conditions by combining **quantitative price discovery with physical trading execution**.

---

## Market Focus

SIP targets **small, fragmented commodity markets** that are typically ignored by large trading houses due to limited scale.

Examples include:

- Regional agricultural commodities  
- Hay and livestock feed markets  
- Agricultural byproducts  
- Scrap and secondary materials  
- Small industrial raw materials  

These markets often exhibit:

- Large information asymmetry  
- Limited price transparency  
- High negotiation spreads  
- Inefficient distribution networks  

---

- Internal commodity price indices  
- Structured pricing methodologies  
- Data pipelines for physical market flows  
- Standardized trade frameworks

This infrastructure improves:

- pricing accuracy  
- counterparty trust  
- execution speed  
- spread capture
